“Check n Mate”: A Hookah Lover’s Paradise in Kolkata

Blow Off Steam and Savor Flavorful Hookahs

Are you ready to embark on a sensory journey that combines relaxation, camaraderie, and tantalizing flavors? Look no further than “Check n Mate,” Kolkata’s ultimate hookah haven. Nestled in the heart of the city, this cozy café promises an unforgettable experience for hookah enthusiasts.


The Ambience

As you step into “Check n Mate,” you’ll be greeted by an inviting ambiance. The dimly lit space, adorned with plush cushions and low tables, sets the perfect mood for winding down. Whether you’re catching up with friends or seeking solace after a long day, this café offers a tranquil escape.

"Where fragrant clouds weave tales, and flavors dance in harmony—Check n Mate is where my soul finds its sweet escape."Avisek Ghosh
“In the fragrant haze of ‘Check n Mate,’ time slows down, and worries fade away—each puff a whispered secret shared between friends.” Shalini Deb

The Hookah Menu

“Check n Mate” takes pride in its extensive hookah menu. Here, you’ll find an array of flavors that cater to every palate:

Two Apple: A classic favorite, combining the sweetness of apple with a hint of anise.

Gum & Supari: A unique blend that combines the sweetness of gum with the aromatic essence of supari (betel nut). It’s a conversation starter for sure!

Grape & Mint: The refreshing tang of grape paired with the coolness of mint—perfect for unwinding after a long day.

Double Apple & Mint: A classic choice! The crispness of double apple meets the invigorating touch of mint. It’s a timeless favorite.

Gum & Cardamom: For those who appreciate a hint of spice, this combination balances gum’s sweetness with the warmth of cardamom.

Blueberry & Spring Water: Dive into the fruity goodness of blueberries, complemented by the purity of spring water.

Brain Freeze Up: Brace yourself for a minty explosion! This flavor will leave you feeling refreshed and rejuvenated.

Strawberry & Watermelon: Juicy strawberries meet the summery vibes of watermelon. It’s like a fruity paradise in every puff.

Paan Ras: A nod to tradition, this paan-flavored hookah is both aromatic and soothing.

Remember, each hookah flavor at “Check n Mate” is crafted with care, ensuring a memorable experience for every guest. So sit back, relax, and savor these delightful concoctions amidst the fragrant clouds.

📍 Location: Check n Mate, Kolkata
