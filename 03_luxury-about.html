Our Story

About Us

At "Check n mate", where every dish tells a story and every sip evokes a sense of delight. We think that creating experiences is more important than creating food. Located in the center of Kolkata, our business is a foodie's paradise for coffee lovers and anybody looking for a cozy setting in which to enjoy delicious food.

Our Story

From Corporate Life to dream cafe. We visited cafes while at work to gather appropriate thoughts. We discovered that, despite the routine of corporate life, we were longing for a closer bond with our communities and hobbies. We set out on an entrepreneurial journey, escaping the restrictions of our office workstations to realize our ideal café, driven by a passion for delicious food and genuine hospitality. With determination and a sprinkle of creativity, we transformed our vision into reality, opening our doors to a world of flavor, friendship, and fulfillment. Every stage of this journey, from the first time we made coffee to the joyous evenings spent with our customers, has served as a monument to the strength of pursuing our dreams.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become more than just a restaurant; we want to be the center of the community, a place where families come together, friends get together, and memories are created. We work hard to exceed expectations with each meal we serve by committing to premium ingredients, faultless service, and unique taste. Checkmate was founded with the goal of redefining the dining experience and starting a passion for exquisite cuisine. What was once only a fantasy has grown into a welcoming, inventive, and genuine dining destination.

Our Cuisine

Our menu is a combination of flavors designed to entice your taste buds and delight your senses, drawing inspiration from many global cuisines. Everything from exquisite Chinese, Mughlai and rich desserts to hearty appetizers selections and more to exclusive Hookah flavors, there's something for everyone to enjoy.

Our Ambiance

Step into our cozy café and you'll be taken to a charming and soothing world. Whether you're spending time with friends or spending some quiet time by yourself, our area invites you to relax, connect, and enjoy the moment with its warm lighting, cozy seats, and colorful décor.

Our Team

A group of enthusiastic people committed to giving our guests experiences they won't soon forget is behind every dish and every cup of coffee. Our dedication to quality and hospitality unites all of us, from our friendly waitstaff to our creative chefs. Our exclusive head chef is Milan Bera.
At Checkmate, we believe that great food has the power to bring people together, create memories, and nourish the soul. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a regular patron, we invite you to join us on this culinary adventure and experience the magic of Checkmate. Cheers to good food, good company, and unforgettable moments!
